Safety Issues and Hazards

Steep decline when cutting in amongst tall bush scrub.
Tripping hazards, snakes, ants etc.
When working on the roadside, please wear high visibility vest.

Cutting techniques

Recut all regrowth trees to ground level or if pollarded before cut flush back to stump.

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Neatly pile offcuts in scrub, try and avoid making tripping hazards.

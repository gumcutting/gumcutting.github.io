Safety Issues and Hazards

Tripping hazards, some steep tracks.
Snakes, ants etc.
No entry in wet conditions as some tracks are clay, no entry on extreme heat days.

Cutting techniques

Along track edges please cut everything low, or if been cut before cut flush back to stump.
Under powerlines, please cut trees at ground level. As close to the ground as possible to avoid creating tripping hazards.
If you find trees that have been pollarded, follow suit and repollard if they are ready.

Safety Issues and Hazards

Tripping hazards, working on a slope, snakes, ants etc.

Please beware of a beautiful Labrador who may come out to say ‘Hello’, not to run him over.

Please leave Brian’s gate as you find it.

Cutting techniques

Cut all gum regrowth back to ground level or back to original stumps.

The Sugar gums to the west of the house can be cut for the birds if needed.

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Please stack offcuts in neat piles out of the way as not to cause tripping hazards.

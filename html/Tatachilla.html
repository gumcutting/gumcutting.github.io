Safety Issues and Hazards

Lots of ants, not nasty ants but will climb your legs and occasionally bite.

Sesonal influences

All season cutting. A regular koala favourite area. Really deluxe delicious gum in spring

Cutting techniques

Red area, continue to prune pollarded trees and re pollard when leaves are ready to cut. Manna area, continue to purine the rows of Manna gums to make it easier to get through. When the tree circumference of the new Manna gum plantation gets to approx. 20 cm, we can begin to pollard the Manna gum trees.
1 or 2 gum cutting staff

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Please pile all wood offcuts into parallel piles at the end of rows, so the piles can be picked up by front end loader forks. Slashing, wood debris collection and burning of piles is carried out by the College maintenance team.

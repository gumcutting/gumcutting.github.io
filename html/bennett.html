    Seasonal Influences
    Can become very wet if entering from bottom gate.

    Safety Issues and Hazards
    Working on slopes, lots of wood debris, tripping hazards, snakes, ants etc.

    Cutting techniques
    Red gum, there may be some opportunistic redgum saplings popping up through the pine plantations. When cutting please be sure to cut saplings flush to ground to minimise gripping hazards.
    Tassie Blue gums, please cut back to ground level. Or if already pollarded please repollard.
    S.A Blue gum, at this stage no cutting in this plot, waiting on direction by PIRSA.
    2 gum cutting staff

    Maintenance Requirements Overview
    Please neatly pile lager wood and offcuts into piles. Please try avoid making further tripping hazards.

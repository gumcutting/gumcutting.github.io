Cutting techniques

In all sections, the pollarded trees are to be repolladed when the branches are big enough, new trees that have grown to approx. 20cm diameter can be pollarded. Please be sure to pollard branches flush to the stump. 1 or 2 gum cutting staff.

Cutting techniques

Continue to pollard taller trees once they have grown to approx. 20 cm diameter.Continue to repollard pollarded trees once the branches are large enough.

1 to 2 gum cutting staff

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Please neatly pile larger wood offcuts at end of the rows, if too large please pile them along the tree rows so a slasher can still get through. The 2020 planting needs to be carefully slashed around each sapling and each sapling weeded and checked to see how many have survived so far. For the plan to do an infill planting in 2022 of any dead saplings. Annual Maintenance: May through to October – burn off any wood and debris piles Oct/Nov – Slashing whole site

Seasonal Influences
No entry when wet or during extreme heat.

Safety Issues and Hazards
Uneven ground, tripping hazards, walking through head height scrub, snakes, inch and hopper ants.
No entry during wet times of the year as Morialta has phytophthora.
When leaving the Morialta site please scrub tyres and boots with a scrubbing brush and spray methylated spirits to help reduce spread.

Cutting techniques
Cut all sapling regrowth flush to the ground to help reduce tripping hazards.
Trees that have originally been originally pollards, continue to prune as a pollarded tree.

Please pile any off cuts neatly in the scrub or on the side of the tracks.

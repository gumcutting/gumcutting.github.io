Safety Issues and Hazards

Snakes, caterpillars, ants, etc.

Cutting techniques

Please prune the lower branches on all the Red and Blue gum rows to make it easier for the slasher to get through the tracks and to promote the trees to grow straight up.
1 or 2 gum cutting staff

Seasonal Influences

No entry during wet winters and on extreme hot weather days

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Please put any smaller offcuts in the middle of the rows to be slashed by the slasher. Any larger wood, please stack neatly parallel in the rows.

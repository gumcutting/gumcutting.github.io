These areas will need to be monitored for the regrowth after the bushfire and when Cleland can resume harvesting gum from these areas.

    Safety Issues and Hazards

    Tripping hazards, some steep tracks.
    Snakes, ants etc.
    No entry in wet conditions as some tracks are clay, no entry on extreme heat days.

    Cutting techniques

    In all sections, the pollarded trees are to be repolladed when the branches are big enough, new trees that have grown to approx. 20cm diameter can be pollarded. Please be sure to pollard branches flush to the stump.
    1 or 2 gum cutting staff.

    Maintenance Requirements Overview

    Please pile all offcuts neatly in the scrub to breakdown.

Safety Issues and Hazards
Tripping hazards, snakes, ants etc.
Please be mindful that sometimes forestry will poison saplings if we haven’t got there to cut them first. Please be guided by the Forest ranger for this information.

Cutting techniques
Please ensure that the red gum saplings are cut flush to the ground to minimise tripping hazards.

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Nursery loop needs the area to be redone, burnout, large fur bushes removed.
Long term this area could be a project to apply for funding to improve the area, it is in a Conservation Park with non-indigenous species spilling into the park as well as being full of weeds.
With a remodel, this area can be a productive close koala feed plantation.

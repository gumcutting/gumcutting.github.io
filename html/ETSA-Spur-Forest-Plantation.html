Cutting techniques

The E.saligna and E.grandis grew very large and the Cleland koalas do not tend to eat these two species. These large trees were pollarded in 2021 and we are still waiting to see how they respond to this.

Maintenance Requirements Overview

The younger saplings need to be looked after, gently weeded around inside the tree guards and the whole section needs to be whipper snipped in late Spring/early Summer.

Cutting techniques
Please continue to prune and re pollard trees when ready. Cut our red gum saplings from the Manna gum rows, during spring this is a delicious source of Red tip.
2 gum cutting staff

Maintenance Requirements Overview
Pile all offcuts in to piles at end of rows or in open grassland in readiness to burn.
Kuipto still needs a lot of work to completely develop the established trees into a working plantation.
Large wood needs to be removed and debris piles need to be burnt off.
Red saplings coming up between the Mann Gum rows need to be cut off at ground level.

Section 1 and 2 still have some very large trees that need to be pollarded. Before these trees can be pollarded the red saplings need to be cleared to allow the light onto the freshly pollarded stumps.

Ultimately organising Kuipto plantation volunteer group during the months of April and November could assist with getting this plantation improved.

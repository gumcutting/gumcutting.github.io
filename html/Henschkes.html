Safety Issues and Hazards

Dropping larger trees, ants, snakes.

Seasonal Influences

Only cut here when favourable weather conditions. No access when ground is very wet. No access on high fire danger days.

Cutting techniques

Cutting large trees off at ground level and some we are pollarding. We are yet to determine which is the best method of cutting for this species of trees as it new to our gum resources.
Over the next 12 months we will be observing how efficiently each cutting style reshoots.
2 gum cutting staff.

Maintenance Requirements Overview

Please look for koala perching, forks and parallels when dropping the larger trees. Once the perches have been cut, they can be left where they are and labelled with some marking tape which should be in Gum 1.
Please pile remaining wood that can be used for firewood in a pile (for ease of firwood collection) and the straggly, stick waste into another pile.

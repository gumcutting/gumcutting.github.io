Safety Issues and Hazards

Tripping hazards, snakes, ants etc

Seasonal Influences

All year cutting, except no entry if extremely wet and at times when the vineyards are being sprayed or during harvest when there is an increase in activity in the vineyards. Please be guided by Danny for this information.

Cutting techniques

Please re cut all trees that have been cut before flush back to stumps. When cutting in the Conservation Park we do not pollard.
If you come across a pollard tree, follow suit and cut back to pollarded stump.

Maintenance Requirements Overview
